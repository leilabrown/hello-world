Hey, how's it going? My name is Leila, and I'm from Eastern Washington. I spend my time reading, playing piano and volleyball, cooking, journaling, listening to music (follow me on Spotify!), and hiking.

My sister Elsina and I at Palouse Falls a couple of months ago.

Every summer (other than this one), I go to a summer camp in Cheney, WA called Satori. I started going 4 years ago, and I've made so many friends and great memories. We get to hang out on Eastern Washington University campus, take classes about stuff like judo, self care, and Star Wars, and spend time at the REC center (ice skating, swimming, wallyball, soccer, rock climbing, etc). It's so much fun. Unfortunately, it was cancelled this year because of COVID-19 :(((
